# Customer Segmentation using Clustering

This project focuses on performing customer segmentation using unsupervised learning techniques (mainly K-Means Clustering) to group similar customers together based on their purchasing behavior and demographics. The goal is to help businesses understand their customer base better and make data-driven marketing or product decisions.
